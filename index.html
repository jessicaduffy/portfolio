About Me

I'm a maker at heart — someone who believes that how things are created matters just as much as what is created.

With a background in business and marketing and a growing expertise in fashion and design management, I'm passionate about building a more thoughtful, sustainable fashion industry — from the ground up. I'm particularly drawn to sourcing and merchandising as spaces where ethics, aesthetics, and strategy intersect.

Currently based in Madrid, I'm immersing myself in the global fashion landscape while sharpening my creative and analytical skills at UPM's master's program in Technology and Innovation in Fashion Design.
